About

Anastasia Rose is a London-based makeup artist specialising in fashion, beauty, celebrity and editorial work. She is known for her refined skin, clean beauty and high-impact creative looks, across all skin tones.

With experience backstage at Fashion Week, Anastasia has worked with the AOFM Pro Team at London Fashion Week and Milan Fashion Week. Graduate of AOFM Pro Artist Training Programme.

Trusted by agencies and clients. Available worldwide.
